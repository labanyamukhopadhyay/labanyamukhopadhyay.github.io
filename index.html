about me

I am a current freshman at UC Berkeley studying computer science and a Regents' and Chancellor's Scholar. I am passionate about technology and stem inclusiviy.

I was introduced to the world of tech through robotics and science fairs. I've done science projects every year from 1st grade to the end of high school, culminating in me receiving an award in the Intel International Science and Engineering Fair. I also founded my nonprofit organization Hellobots in 2015 to share my love of STEM to children without access to it. The program has reached over 1000 students across the Bay Area. In my free time, I enjoy discovering aesthetic cafes nearby and writing (blog coming soon!)
